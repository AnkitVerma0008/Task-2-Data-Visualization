# Task 2 - Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
Create an interactive dashboard using the Superstore Sales dataset in Power BI and present business insights through visualizations.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Superstore Sales Dataset

## Dashboard Features
- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Average Sales KPI
- Sales by Product Category
- Profit by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Region Filter
- Product Category Filter

## Key Business Insights
- Identified the highest-selling product category.
- Compared profit across different regions.
- Analyzed monthly sales trends.
- Evaluated customer segment contributions.
- Highlighted the top 10 best-selling products.
